High Quality Education - 13 Universities in Top 200

Netherlands has 13 universities ranked in world's top 200. Globally recognized degrees with excellent career prospects.

Work While Studying in Netherlands

Indian students can work 16 hours per week during studies and full-time during summer holidays to support living expenses.

🏆

1-Year Post-Study Work Visa (Search Year)

Graduates get 1-year orientation year visa to find employment in Netherlands. Excellent opportunity for Indian students to gain European work experience.

Excellent Quality of Life

Safe, multicultural society with excellent public transportation, English-speaking population, and vibrant international student community.
